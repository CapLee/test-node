About SILVER

Station of Interactive Learning & Virtual Education Resources (SILVER) is an online learning platform under the QESS project "A Learning Model in the Digital Age: Interactive Learning and Assessment Platform for Science Courses" held by the College of International Education, HKBU.

SILVER aims to：

▪ Provide a supplemental way to enhance students' learning of fundamental concepts and skills of science courses.

▪ Arouse students' interest in studying science courses.

Acknowledgement：

The Project was supported by Quality Enhancement Support Scheme (QESS) grant from the Education Bureau of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region, China (Project No. 02/QESS/2019).
